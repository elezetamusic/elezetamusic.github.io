Hi,

I started playing (with) synthesizers a few years ago and I had already piled up many drafts and demos. I finally put together the courage and willpower to go through them and savage and polish the pieces that sounded somewhat interesting.

The result is this album that contains ten tracks of electronic music that, even though they are simple in their setup and melody, I've considered they feature an interesting soundscape.

If you like my music or want to follow me on this journey, follow or contact me through my Facebook page.

Thanks!
