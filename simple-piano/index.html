Hi,

I never had a formal education in music and I was never able to read music at a reasonable speed, but I've spent years improvising at the piano and recording improvisation sessions.

This album is the result of going through many recorded and unfocused improvisation sessions between 2013 and 2020, identifying several fragments that were memorable, interesting, or challenging, and editing them into simple piano tracks.

If you like my music or want to follow me on this journey, follow or contact me through my Facebook page.

Thanks!

Elezeta
