Hi,

In Shelter, I learned and exercised songwriting in English.

Now comes Altered Times, an experiment on more complex production: cleaner vocals, more complex rock-like arrangements, and better mixing and mastering.

You can also listen to the backing tracks here.

If you like my music or want to follow me on this journey, follow or contact me through my Facebook page.

Thanks!

Elezeta
