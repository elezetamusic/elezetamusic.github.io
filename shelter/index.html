Hi,

In my previous project, Cocodrilos y Mapaches, I learned and exercised the basics of songwriting in my mother tongue, and recording, editing, mixing, and mastering music.

Now comes Shelter, an experiment on writing songs in English. Shelter is an indie easy-listening album that started with me writing thirty songs in two weeks, then choosing the best seven, and polishing and producing them over two months.

Note that I have licensed some of the songs in this album. Check below for more information.

If you like my music or want to follow me on this journey, follow or contact me through my Facebook page.

Thanks!

Elezeta
